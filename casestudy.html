Case Study: HR System Platform

This application is designed to accommodate company processes in a care home which are still carried out offline. This application contains several features so that end-to-end users can manage staff data, leave, payroll and more online using the application.

Background

In the care home, the Staff are aged between 24yrs to 55yrs with 70% of them being older individuals and 30% being younger. This as sparked the author's interest in designing an application that facilitate easier online management of HR processes for users aged between 37 - 55.

Problem

  • Still using the offline method.
  • Work hours are not properly recorded.
  • Users cannot see the progress of their 'Leave of Absence.'
  • Users can not update their employment details.
  • Payslip are not available thus not being able to see if they were under-paid or taxed higher.
  • Staffs being the last to know about events, changes in the companyâ€™s policies and operations.

Solution

The solution that is made is to build an application that can foster the smooth running and management of the organization so that business processes for end-to-end users can occur properly.

  • Creation of online HR support feature.
  • Creation of employee details.
  • User can see the progress of their leave request.
  • Catalog that can inform the user of company events, benefits and career progression
  • Users can view their paylips.
  • Rotas are made available, and worked hours can be recorded and viewed.
